Money Plant

The Pothos N’Joy (Epipremnum aureum 'N’Joy') is a striking indoor plant loved for its compact growth habit and creamy white variegated leaves. Thriving in bright, indirect light, it maintains its vibrant patterns while tolerating lower-light spaces, though variegation may fade. It prefers a well-draining potting mix made from 60% high-quality soil, 20% perlite, and 20% coco peat or peat moss to balance aeration and moisture. Ideal growing conditions include temperatures between 18°C–29°C and moderate to high humidity, with occasional misting in drier climates. Water when the top 1–2 inches of soil are dry, reducing frequency during winter to prevent root rot.
